Common Plant Problems

Quick solutions for the most frequent plant issues

Yellowing Leaves

Overwatering, nutrient deficiency, or natural aging can cause yellow leaves.

Pest Infestation

Aphids, spider mites, and scale insects can damage your plants.

Root Rot

Overwatering leads to fungal infections that damage root systems.

Brown Leaf Tips

Low humidity, fluoride in water, or over-fertilization causes brown tips.

Wilting Plants

Underwatering, root problems, or shock can cause plants to wilt.

Slow Growth

Poor lighting, nutrient deficiency, or wrong pot size affects growth.

Seasonal Care Tips

Adapt your plant care routine throughout the year

Spring Care

  • • Increase watering frequency
  • • Start fertilizing schedule
  • • Repot root-bound plants
  • • Prune dead growth
  • • Move plants to brighter spots

Summer Care

  • • Water more frequently
  • • Provide shade from harsh sun
  • • Increase humidity levels
  • • Monitor for pests
  • • Move plants outdoors

Fall Care

  • • Reduce watering gradually
  • • Stop fertilizing
  • • Bring outdoor plants inside
  • • Check for pest hitchhikers
  • • Prepare for dormancy

Winter Care

  • • Water less frequently
  • • Provide supplemental lighting
  • • Increase humidity indoors
  • • Avoid cold drafts
  • • Monitor for dry air damage
