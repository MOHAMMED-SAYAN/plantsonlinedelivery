About PlantHub

Passionate plant experts dedicated to bringing nature into your home. Discover our story, meet our team, and learn why thousands trust us for their plant care journey.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by botanical enthusiasts Sarah Chen and Marcus Rodriguez, PlantHub began as a small greenhouse operation with a simple mission: make plant care accessible to everyone.

What started as weekend farmers market visits has grown into a comprehensive plant care ecosystem, serving over 50,000 plant parents across the country. Our journey from a 500-square-foot greenhouse to a 15,000-square-foot facility reflects our commitment to quality and education.

Our Mission

To cultivate a world where everyone can experience the joy and benefits of plant parenthood through expert guidance, premium plants, and ongoing support.
